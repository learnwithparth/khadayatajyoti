Ekda-wise Members

Members of the Khadayata Samaj organised by their Ekda (regional community group)

What is an Ekda?

The Ekda System in Khadayata Samaj

An Ekda is a sub-group within the Khadayata Samaj, typically based on the geographic origin of families — the town or village from which they originally hailed. The Ekda system helps maintain community cohesion and facilitates local organisation of events, marriages, and welfare activities.

Members are listed under their respective Ekda for easier community connection. If you are unsure of your Ekda, contact the Karyalay for assistance.
