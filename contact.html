Organisers

Khadayata Jyoti — Organisers

The dedicated team behind Khadayata Jyoti magazine and community portal

👨‍💼

Editorial Committee

The editorial committee of Khadayata Jyoti oversees the magazine's content — curating articles, community news, religious guidance, and member announcements for each issue.

🏛

Managing Committee

The managing committee handles administration, membership, finances, and the overall operations of the Khadayata Jyoti organisation and Karyalay.

🎓

Education Committee

The Education Committee (Kelavani Mandal) reviews scholarship and loan applications, selects awardees, and manages the disbursement of educational support to deserving students.
