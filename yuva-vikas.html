Yuva Vikas Margdarshan

Nurturing the next generation of Khadayata leaders through education, scholarships, and career guidance

Investing in Our Youth — The Community's Future

The Khadayata Samaj has always believed that education is the key to community advancement. Since the founding of the Khadayata Kelavani Mandal (Education Society) in 1916, the community has invested heavily in the education and development of its young members.

Yuva Vikas Margdarshan — which translates to "Guidance for Youth Development" — is the umbrella programme under which the Khadayata Jyoti and the Khadayata Samaj coordinate all youth-focused initiatives. This includes financial aid, career guidance, recognition of academic excellence, and platforms for youth participation in community life.

We believe that a community that nurtures its youth secures its future. Every scholarship awarded, every loan disbursed, and every young achiever recognised is an investment in the Khadayata community's next century.

Kelavani Mandal — Est. 1916

A Century of Educational Support

Founded in 1916, the Khadayata Education Society (Kelavani Mandal) was established specifically to provide financial support to deserving students within the community.

Over its century-long history, it has provided scholarships, interest-free loans, and boarding facilities — enabling thousands of Khadayata students to achieve higher education.

Our Programmes

Youth Development Programmes

Comprehensive support for Khadayata youth at every stage of their educational journey

🎓

Shishyavruti (Scholarship)

Merit and need-based scholarships for Khadayata students pursuing higher education. Available for students from Class 10 through postgraduate and professional degrees (Engineering, Medicine, Law, Commerce, Arts, Science).

Applications accepted annually. Priority given to students with academic excellence and financial need.

Apply via Shishyavruti Form
💰

Education Loan (Interest-Free)

Interest-free educational loans for Khadayata students who need financial support beyond scholarships. Loans are available for tuition, books, hostel accommodation, and related educational expenses.

Repayable after completion of education. No interest charged — a community service, not a commercial loan.

Apply via Loan Form
🏆

Tejasvi Student Award

The prestigious Tejasvi Student Award recognises the most outstanding academic achievers among Khadayata youth. Students who score exceptionally in board exams and university examinations are nominated and honoured at the Parishad.

Nominees are published in Khadayata Jyoti and recognised in a community ceremony.

Apply via Tejasvi Form
🏠

Khadayata Bhavan (Student Housing)

The Khadayata Bhavan community centres in major cities provide affordable accommodation for Khadayata students studying in Ahmedabad, Baroda, Bombay, and other cities away from home.

Priority given to students from smaller towns and villages pursuing education in major cities.

Contact Karyalay for Details
🧭

Career Guidance & Margdarshan

Expert career counselling and guidance for Khadayata youth at the crossroads of education and career decisions. Mentorship from experienced professionals within the community who volunteer their time to guide the next generation.

Organised at Parishad events and through dedicated guidance sessions.

Enquire via Contact Page
👩‍💼

Women's Education & Empowerment

Special scholarships and support programmes for Khadayata girls and women pursuing higher education and professional careers. The Mahila Mandal coordinates skill-building workshops and vocational training programmes.

The community is committed to equal educational opportunity for all members, regardless of gender.

Contact Mahila Mandal
Eligibility & How to Apply

How to Apply for Scholarships

Step-by-step guide for Khadayata students to access financial support

1️⃣
Check Eligibility

You must be a member of the Khadayata community, enrolled in a recognised educational institution, and meet the academic and financial criteria. Both merit-based and need-based options are available.

2️⃣
Download the Appropriate Form

Download the Shishyavruti Form (for scholarships), Loan Form (for education loans), or Tejasvi Student Form (for award nominations) from the Forms page.

3️⃣
Complete the Form

Fill in the form completely with your personal details, educational information, marks/grades, income details (for need-based), and attach required documents (mark sheets, income certificate, Khadayata community certificate, etc.).

4️⃣
Submit to the Karyalay

Submit your completed form and documents to the Khadayata Jyoti Karyalay before the application deadline. Applications are typically accepted between June and September for the academic year.

5️⃣
Selection & Announcement

The selection committee reviews all applications. Results are announced at the community Parishad and published in the Khadayata Jyoti magazine. Awarded students are publicly recognised.
