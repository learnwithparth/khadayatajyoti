Shishyavruti Form (Scholarship Application)

For Khadayata students seeking financial scholarship support for higher education

Who Can Apply?

  • Students belonging to the Khadayata community
  • Enrolled in a recognised educational institution
  • Pursuing Class 10 onwards — graduation, postgraduate, or professional degrees
  • Students with financial need (need-based) or academic merit (merit-based)
  • Must have passed the previous year's examination

Documents Required

  • Latest mark sheet / result
  • Community certificate (Khadayata)
  • Income certificate (for need-based)
  • School / college admission proof
  • Passport-size photograph
  • Bank account details (student's or parent's)
ℹ Scholarship applications are accepted annually between June and September. Incomplete applications will not be considered. Decisions of the Education Committee are final.

Loan Form (Interest-Free Education Loan)

For Khadayata students requiring interest-free loans for higher education expenses

About the Education Loan

  • Interest-free loan — no interest charged, ever
  • Available for tuition fees, books, hostel, and related expenses
  • Repayable after completion of education, in manageable instalments
  • For Khadayata community members in financial need
  • Amount determined by the Education Committee based on need and availability

Documents Required

  • Completed loan form with all sections filled
  • Community certificate (Khadayata)
  • Income proof of family (latest year)
  • Educational institution admission letter
  • Fee structure from the institution
  • Guarantor details (a Khadayata community member)
  • Passport-size photograph
ℹ Loans are disbursed to students in genuine financial need. Repayment schedules are arranged after discussion. Members are expected to repay so future students can also benefit.

Tejasvi Student Form (Award Nomination)

For nominating outstanding Khadayata student achievers for the prestigious Tejasvi Student Award

About the Tejasvi Student Award

  • Recognises the highest academic achievers among Khadayata youth
  • Awarded at the annual Khadayata Parishad — a prestigious community honour
  • Recipients are published by name in Khadayata Jyoti magazine
  • Open to students from Class 10 onwards who achieve outstanding marks
  • Students who secure distinctions in board exams, university examinations, or professional courses

How to Nominate

  • Download and complete the Tejasvi Student Form
  • Attach mark sheets / result documents
  • Parent or guardian must certify the application
  • Submit before the nomination deadline (announced in the magazine)
  • Self-nominations and parent nominations are both accepted
🏅 The Tejasvi Award is a community honour — nominated students are celebrated at the Parishad and inspire the next generation of Khadayata youth.

Membership Form

Register as a member of the Khadayata Jyoti Samaj and receive the magazine and member benefits

Membership Tiers & Fees

  • Annual Membership — ₹500: One-year membership, full benefits
  • Life Membership — ₹2,500: Permanent listing in member directory, magazine subscription for life
  • Patron — ₹10,000: Community patron recognition
  • Special Patron — ₹25,000: Premier recognition in magazine and events
  • Gnyati Ratna — ₹1,50,000: The highest community honour

Membership Benefits

  • Regular Khadayata Jyoti magazine issues
  • Listing in the community member directory
  • Access to Ekda-wise member network
  • Eligibility for scholarship and loan programmes
  • Invitations to Parishad and community events
  • Free matrimonial listing in magazine (Patron and above)
🏦 Bank Details: Pay your membership fee directly to the Khadayata Jyoti bank account. Contact the Karyalay at info@khadayatajyoti.org for the current bank account number, IFSC code, and UPI payment details.
